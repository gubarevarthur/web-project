Synoptic map

A synoptic map is a geographical map on which the results of observations at a network of meteorological stations at certain points in time are plotted with numbers and symbols. Such charts are regularly compiled by the weather service several times a day; their analysis is the main operation enabling the subsequent weather forecast.
